🙏 About Lord Dattatreya

Lord Dattatreya is the divine incarnation of the Holy Trinity — Brahma, Vishnu, and Mahesh. He symbolizes the unity of creation, preservation, and transformation. His teachings inspire simplicity, service, and self-realization. The temple stands as a beacon of peace and spiritual awakening.
